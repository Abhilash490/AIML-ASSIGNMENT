Features (Inputs)
Presence of spam words (e.g., “free”, “win”, “offer”)
Message length
Number of links
Sender information
Frequency of special characters (!, $, etc.)
Data Needed
Labeled dataset of messages (Spam / Not Spam)
Large variety of real-world messages
Balanced dataset to avoid bias
Possible Mistakes
False Positive: Important message marked as spam
False Negative: Spam message not detected
Overfitting if trained on limited data
Language variations may confuse model
New spam patterns may not be recognized
