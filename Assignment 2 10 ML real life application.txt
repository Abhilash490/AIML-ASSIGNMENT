1. Voice Assistants (Google Assistant / Siri)

Uses speech recognition models to convert voice into text and NLP to understand and respond to user queries.

2. YouTube Recommendations

ML analyzes watch history, likes, and behavior to suggest videos using recommendation algorithms.

3. Google Maps Navigation

Uses ML to predict traffic conditions and suggest the fastest route based on real-time and historical data.

4. Email Spam Filter

ML models classify emails as spam or not by learning patterns from previously marked spam messages.

5. Netflix / OTT Suggestions

Recommends movies and shows using collaborative filtering based on user preferences and viewing patterns.

6. Online Shopping (Amazon / Flipkart)

Suggests products using ML by analyzing browsing history, purchases, and user interests.

7. Face Unlock (Smartphone)

Uses computer vision and deep learning to recognize and match facial features for authentication.

8. Chatbots (Customer Support)

NLP-based ML models understand user queries and provide automated responses or solutions.

9. Social Media Feed (Instagram / Facebook)

ML ranks posts based on engagement, likes, and interactions to show personalized content.

10. Keyboard Autocorrect & Suggestions

Uses language models to predict and correct words based on typing patterns and context.
